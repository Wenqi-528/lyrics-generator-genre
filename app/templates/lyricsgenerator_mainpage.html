Frequently Asked Questions

How does it work?
  • –
    Our lyric generator is fairly simple to use. You start by typing in a phrase to begin your lyrics, such as "a love so". Then just run the program. As a result, the lyrics generator will return three different lyrics beginning with "a love so"!
What genres can I make lyrics for?
  • –
    You can generate lyrics for three different genres. These genres are rock, jazz, and hip hop!
How do I turn my lyrics into a song?
  • –
    To create a song that flows using the lyrics generator, you can simply start by running the model once. Once, you have created your first line you can copy that lyric into generator again to get the next line. These lyrics should flow together to create a beautiful song for you!
